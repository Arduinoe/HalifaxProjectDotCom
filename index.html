about

halifax

Halifax is a newly formed design practice examining the possibilities of industrial techniques and emerging materials. Founded by Arduino Massimo Lancellotti (@tauroboyprince), it began as a personal project to shape an ideal workspace. This practical starting point soon evolved into a broader inquiry into the dialogue between design, fabrication, and everyday life. Rather than claiming mastery, Halifax approaches each creation as a learning step, encouraging experimentation and embracing the unexpected.

For inquiries DM us on instagram: @halifaxproject
